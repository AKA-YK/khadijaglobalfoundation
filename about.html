About Us

We are a global non-profit dedicated to uplifting communities and protecting the vulnerable.

Who We Are

Khadija Global Foundation was founded with a simple but powerful vision: to create a world where every person and every living being has access to care, dignity, and opportunity.

From food distribution and education to animal rescue and emergency relief, we work across 25+ countries, driven by compassion and accountability.

Our team of 300+ volunteers and staff are committed to transparency and long-term impact.
