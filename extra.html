About Khadija Global Foundation

Khadija Global Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to helping poor families, supporting orphan children, rescuing animals, and providing humanitarian assistance around the world.

Our goal is to create a future where every person and every living creature has access to care, compassion, and hope.

Learn More

Our Mission

Together we create hope, dignity, and a better future for people and animals.

❤️

Helping Poor Families

We provide food, clothing, shelter, and financial support to families living in poverty.

🐾

Animal Welfare

We rescue injured, abandoned, and homeless animals while providing food and medical care.

📚

Education & Healthcare

We support children's education and provide medical assistance to those who cannot afford treatment.
